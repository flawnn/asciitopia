# Attribution

asciitopia's patterns draw on the work of some wonderful terminal-art projects.
As of v0.1, asciitopia contains no code or art copied from these projects.
They're credited here as inspiration:

## cbonsai by John Allbritten (jallbrit)
https://gitlab.com/jallbrit/cbonsai (GPL-3.0)

The bonsai pattern's premise — a procedural tree growing character by
character — is inspired by cbonsai. asciitopia's growth model, branch and
leaf art, pots, and tuning are original and were written independently.

## weathr by Veirt
https://github.com/veirt/weathr (GPL-3.0)

The weather pattern's premise — a cozy scene living under real weather —
is inspired by weathr. asciitopia's scene art, weather systems, ground
generator, and tuning are original and were written independently.
